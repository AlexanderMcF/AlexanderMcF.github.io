Potential Areas of Improvement

Adding an IP Allowed List

Areas of the CIA triad affected: Confidentiality

To keep your website confidential, we recommend utilizing an IP allowed list, also known as a "whitelist." This can help ensure that only authorized users have access to the website you have created.


Utilizing Backups

Areas of the CIA triad affected: Integrity, Availability

Backups are incredibly important to have in the event of data loss. By utilizing backups, you ensure that your webserver has minimum downtime in the event of an attack or system failure. By utilizing backups, you ensure that the data is both readily available to access and maintain the integrity of the data that could've been potentially lost.

How often you use backups is dependent on the type of webserver you have and how impactful any amount of potential downtime might be to the service you are providing.
